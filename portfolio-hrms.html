Project information

  • Category: Web System
  • Client: Kinetic Phils. Electrical Construction, Inc. (Ainpex Solutions Corporation's Client)
  • Project date: 2023
  • Project URL: Cannot disclose
  • Description: This system is designed to optimize HR processes and enhance employee management as well as their payroll and reports management.

Key Features Include:

  • Dashboard

    1. Headcount Analytics: Visualize employee demographics with pie charts displaying headcount by employment type (regular, contractual, probationary, project based) and gender.
    2. Department Overview: Monitor departmental performance with headcount statistics, showing the number of present employees against total active employees.
    3. Attendance Tracking: Access overall attendance counts and real-time attendance logs to maintain workforce accountability
    4. Employee Milestones: Highlight employment anniversaries and birthdays of active employees for enhanced employee engagement.
    5. Document Management: Track expiring documents for active employees, ensuring compliance and timely renewals.

  • Employees
    1. Employee Registration: Easily register new employees and maintain a comprehensive employee masterlist
    2. Data Management: Search and filter employee data by various criteria (gender, birth month, civil status, department, etc.) and update records as needed.
    3. Document Reprinting: Quickly reprint essentials documents, including company ID cards, employment contracts, onboarding checklist, and FARQ.
    4. Update Requests: Streamline the process for employee update requests and manage organization setups.
    5. ID Generation: Generate and print employee IDs (by department or by employee) with robust logging form tracking.

  • Timekeeping
    1. Leave and Overtime Management: Process overtime requests and leave applications efficiently.
    2. Attendance Logging: Maintain accurate attendance logs and manual log requests to ensure accurate time tracking.
    3. Timesheet Processing: Easily manage and process employee timesheets and work schedules, including holiday setups.

  • Payroll
    1. Comprehensive Payroll Management: Handle payroll deductions, compensations, and employee benefits management.
    2. Payroll Processing: Efficiently process payroll and manage payroll accounts with automated calculations to minimize errors.

  • Reports and Change Logs
    1. Dynamic Reporting: Generate detailed reports on various HR metrics for data-driven decision-making.
    2. Change Logs: Maintain a comprehensive log of changes for transparency and compliance.

  • Permissions
    1. Access Management: Implement role-based access control, ensuring appropriate permissions are assigned to different user groups.

Technologies Used:

  • Back-end: PHP, MySQL
  • Front-end: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (using downloadable bootstrap template)
